Kilometer-Scale ML Weather Forecasting

Research map for kilometer-scale forecasting, downscaling, and global priors

This page is meant to serve as a living repository of research efforts applying machine learning to kilometer-scale weather forecasting. It is organized into three sections:

  1. KM Forecasting Models: True kilometer-scale forecasting systems or regional LAMs.
  2. KM Downscaling & Generative Models: Diffusion, super-resolution, and benchmark efforts delivering km outputs.
  3. Global Drivers & Priors: Global models frequently used as boundary conditions or baselines for km-scale models.
